About Safari Rush Uganda

Safari Rush Uganda is your premier tours and travel company based in Uganda. We specialize in curated experiences like gorilla trekking in Bwindi and wildlife safaris in Queen Elizabeth National Park. Whether you're looking for a custom tour or a pre-planned adventure, let us guide you through the heart of Africa.

Why Travel with Us

Expert Local Guides

Our guides are Ugandan locals with extensive knowledge of the best wildlife spots, cultural sites, and hidden gems, ensuring an authentic experience.

Customizable Tours

Tailor your adventure to your interests—whether it’s wildlife, culture, or relaxation—we design tours that match your preferences.

Sustainable Tourism

We prioritize eco-friendly practices and support local communities, preserving Uganda’s natural and cultural heritage.

Exceptional Customer Service

From your first inquiry to the end of your trip, our team provides personalized support for a seamless journey.

Best Value for Your Money

Enjoy competitive pricing with no hidden costs, delivering high-quality experiences at affordable rates.
