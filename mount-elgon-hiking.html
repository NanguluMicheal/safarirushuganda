Mount Elgon Hiking Expedition in Uganda

Duration: 4 Days | Price: $850 per person

Conquer Mount Elgon on this 4-day hiking expedition, featuring trails through bamboo forests, caves, and moorlands. Reach the Wagagai Peak (4,321m) for breathtaking views, with stops at Sipi Falls for relaxation. Accommodation includes mountain camps and lodges, with porters, guides, and hearty meals provided. A challenging yet rewarding adventure for nature lovers.

Itinerary for Your Mount Elgon Hiking Expedition

Day 1: Arrive in Mbale and transfer to the Mount Elgon base camp. Begin hiking through dense forest trails, overnight in a mountain camp.

Day 2: Hike to the moorland zone, exploring caves and enjoying panoramic views. Overnight in a high-altitude camp.

Day 3: Summit the Wagagai Peak (4,321m) and descend to a lodge near Sipi Falls for a relaxing evening.

Day 4: Visit Sipi Falls for a short hike and photography session, then transfer back to Entebbe.

What’s Included in the Mount Elgon Hiking Package

- Park entry fees and permits
- Accommodation (camps and lodges)
- All meals during the hike
- Expert guides and porters
- Transport to and from Entebbe

What to Bring for Hiking Mount Elgon

Sturdy hiking boots, warm clothing for high altitudes, rain gear, a backpack, and a camera for stunning views.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hiking Mount Elgon

What is the best time to hike Mount Elgon?

The dry seasons (June to August and December to February) offer the best weather for hiking Mount Elgon.

How challenging is the hike to Wagagai Peak?

The hike is moderately challenging, with steep sections and high altitude. A good level of fitness is recommended.
