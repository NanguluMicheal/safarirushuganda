Nile River Adventure

The mighty Nile River offers a unique blend of adventure and relaxation. Join us for a journey that includes serene boat trips and adrenaline-pumping white-water rafting near Jinja, the adventure capital of East Africa.

Activities

  • Boat cruise to the source of the Nile, with opportunities to spot birds and wildlife.
  • White-water rafting on Grade 5 rapids for thrill-seekers.
  • Kayaking and paddleboarding for a more relaxed experience.

What’s Included

Our packages include transportation, meals, and all necessary equipment. We also provide experienced guides to ensure your safety on the water.
