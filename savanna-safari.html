Savanna Safari in Queen Elizabeth National Park

Experience the thrill of a savanna safari in Queen Elizabeth National Park, one of Uganda’s most diverse ecosystems. This park is home to the famous tree-climbing lions, herds of elephants, and countless other species.

Highlights

  • Game drives through the Kasenyi Plains to spot lions and leopards.
  • A boat safari on the Kazinga Channel, where you’ll see hippos, crocodiles, and a variety of birds.
  • Guided nature walks to explore the park’s flora and smaller creatures.

Best Time to Visit

The dry seasons (January to February and June to September) are ideal for game viewing, as animals gather around water sources. However, the wet seasons offer lush landscapes and fewer crowds.
