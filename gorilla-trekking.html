Gorilla Trekking Adventure in Uganda

Duration: 3 Days | Price: $1,200 per person

Embark on a thrilling trek through Bwindi Impenetrable Forest to observe endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. This 3-day adventure includes gorilla permits, expert trackers, and a visit to a local Batwa community. Accommodation is provided in cozy forest lodges, with meals featuring Ugandan cuisine. Highlights include a one-hour gorilla encounter and scenic hikes through lush landscapes.

Itinerary for Your Gorilla Trekking Tour

Day 1: Arrive in Entebbe and transfer to Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. Overnight at a lodge.

Day 2: Full-day gorilla trekking with expert guides. Return to the lodge for dinner and relaxation.

Day 3: Morning cultural visit with the Batwa community, then transfer back to Entebbe.

What’s Included in the Gorilla Trekking Package

- Gorilla trekking permit
- Accommodation and meals
- Transport and guide services
- Cultural experience with the Batwa

What to Bring for Gorilla Trekking in Uganda

Sturdy hiking boots, rain gear, long-sleeve clothing, and a camera to capture the moment.

Frequently Asked Questions About Gorilla Trekking

What is the best time for gorilla trekking in Uganda?

The best time for gorilla trekking in Uganda is during the dry seasons: June to September and December to February. Read more in our wildlife safety tips.

How fit do I need to be for gorilla trekking?

Gorilla trekking can involve several hours of hiking through dense forest. A moderate level of fitness is recommended.
